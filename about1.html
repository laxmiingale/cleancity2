About Clean City

Clean City is a smart garbage complaint reporting system designed to help citizens report waste problems in their area.


Through this platform, people can easily report garbage by uploading photos, writing descriptions and giving location details.


Municipal officers can view complaints, schedule garbage trucks, update complaint status and upload cleaned area photos.


This system helps improve waste management and keeps the city clean, safe and healthy for everyone.


Project Information

Project Name: Clean City – Smart Garbage Complaint System

Team Name: Smart Waste Fighters

Technology Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

This project is developed as part of a Computer Science project to improve city cleanliness and encourage citizen participation.
